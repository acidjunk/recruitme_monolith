Info for recruiters

Intro

Most developers are annoyed when recruiters claim to have the next best job offer that just doesn't make sense. There are also a lot of developers working as a freelancer; they are most of the time not interested in job offers and vice versa. RecruitMeNow was born to out of the desire to create a high quality match in less time.

Developers can connect with heir github account and create a c.v. in very little time. They decide when they want to share it with a recruitment professional
